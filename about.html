hello, my name is aisha kazembe. it is great to see you here. i am an engineer and artist. i currently attend parsons as a masters student in Design and Technology, graduated from the university of southern california with a b.s. in environmental engineering, class of 2022. my work focuses on digital and physical messaging, sustainability, and fabrication with a particular interest in dreams and experiences, and how these concepts interact with sustainability and the built environment. i enjoy working with paint, natural resources, digital systems, and sustainable building design, and have practical experience with environmental data transformation and management and digital system design. in art, i am focused on built and natural environments, people, and how they interact with each other. these interests amalgamate as i pursue a career in collaborative efforts to create built and digital environments that interact harmonically with humans, the natural environment, and the eye.
