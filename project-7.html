the missing clock - data portrait

this iteration of the missing clock adds data into my original missing clock. this data portrait version uses an array of data - which consists of txt messages between my mom and i per day - and translates them as the distance between us as the user clicks through.
the distance is plotted on a much more local scale to help the visuals, compared to the original missing clock. it's plotted on a range between the most and least texts sent on any one day throughout the week. the day that the most texts were sent, the distance is the smallest and the visuals are slightly different. click through to see the week.
